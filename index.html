Projects

HEXchain

Decentralized application for medical records.

This Dapp was developed at HEX Hackathon. I could convince a team to help me build a decentralised platform to give patients control over their data and the ability to monetise it as well. We ended up in the hackathon finalists and won the healthtech track with a Dapp prototype on the Ethereum blockchain where doctors manage patients records and institutes can directly acquire anonymised data from the patients.

I took the risk of being responsible of the blockchain backend, without previous experience. But succeeded in building a dapp using solidity, remix, truffle, metamask, ganache and integrated it with a React frontend.

I Like Fridges

iOS app to manage your fridge based on photos of grocery receipts.

This hack was developed at Hackzurich. The idea was to process the receipts before feeding them to Google OCR API, then parse the results and store them in a data base. The app gave the user realtime informations about his fridge but he had to take stuff out manually :p. It was a cool proof of concept and I had the chance to try React Native for the first time. After this project I got really interested to ML problems and decided to learn Python as well ;)
